Bukayo Saka: Tribute Page

Saka's Arsenal Career Timeline

  • 2010 - Joins Arsenal's Hale End Academy, aged 8
  • 2018 - Offered full-time scholarship forms with Arsenal, aged 16. Makes first-team debut in a UEFA Europa League match against Vorskla Poltava, coming on a 68th minute substitute
  • 2019 - Makes his Premier League debut in Arsenal's 4-1 win against Fulham as an 83rd minute substitue
  • 2020 - Scores his first Premier League goal in a 2-0 win against Wolverhampton Wanderers (Pictured above), aged 19
  • 2022 - Named Arsenal's Player of the Season for the 2nd year running. He became the first player to retain the award since Thiery Henry in 2004
  • 2024 - Registered 16 Premier League goals and 9 assists as Arsenal narrowly lost out on the title to Manchester City after taking their title challenge to the final day of the season
